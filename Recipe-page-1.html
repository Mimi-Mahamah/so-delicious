West African Peanut Stew Recipe

Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min. Serves 4-6.

Ingredients/Instructions

Step 1 - Sauté: In a large pot, heat oil or 4 cups water or low-sodium vegetable broth over medium heat,
add the 1 large onion, 5-6 cloves minced garlic, 2-3 tbsps. grated ginger, and 1-2 diced chili
peppers (jalapeno, serrano, scotch bonnet or habanero peppers) and sauté for 5 minutes stirring frequently.

Step 2 - Boil and simmer: Add 2-3 lbs. sweet potatoes (peeled and cut into ½ inch cubes), 1 can (15 oz.)
diced tomatoes, spices and liquids, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes,
or until sweet potatoes are fork tender.

Step 3 - Add final ingredients: Turn off heat and stir in the ¾ cup natural peanut butter (creamy or smooth),
and finish with 5 oz. fresh spinach (chopped if not using baby spinach) and lemon juice
(2 small lemons). Soup will thicken upon cooling. Season with salt & pepper to taste.

Nutrition Facts: Amount per serving: Calories 276, Protein 15g, Carbohydrate 22.3g, Fat 17.7g,
Cholesterol 0mg, Sodium 201.4mg
